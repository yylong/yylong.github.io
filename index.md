I am currently a lecturer in the [School of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering](http://lxy.gzmu.edu.cn " School of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering") at [Guizhou Minzu University](http://www.gzmu.edu.cn "Guizhou Mingzu University"), and working in the Intelligent Unmanned System Perception and Control Research Group.

My research interests are in the areas of guidance, control systems, and planning algorithms. I am particularly interested in developing advanced control techniques and applications in the air vehicles that make air vehicles to complete tasks under strict conditions. Besides, I am interested in precise states estimation of air vehicles with general and advanced sensors.

Currently, I am focused on modeling and control methods for multi-body under-actuated flight vehicle systems. While previous works always describe the multi-body system with local-coordinates and design controllers in the local chart, our methods provide a much more compact and simpler general formulation to describe multi-body system globally. Based on the modeling method, a guidance and control method with the intrinsic properties of the system itself is provided.
